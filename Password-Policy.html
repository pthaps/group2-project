Full Password Reset Policy

Why is it Important? The password reset policy protects users and the organization, and the policy creates clear requirements for creating strong passwords and resetting them frequently.

What are the Requirements? All users are required by our policy to reset their passwords every 90 days, be unique, and meet all the complexity requirements, length minimum of 12 characters, must have an uppercase character, lowercase character and symbol/special character. Users need to verify their email through a one-time code when resetting their password and additionally must reset their password if they suspect their account may have been compromised.

How do I get Help? If you are not able to comply with the password reset policy because you are unsure of what you need to do. Please contact the IT support, they are available from 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM Monday - Friday at (555)-555-5555.

Are there any Exceptions? Exceptions are allowed for some service accounts and special systems, all exceptions must be approved by the IT department.

When is the policy reviewed? The policy is reviewed twice every year and after any security incidents, and revisions are made if they are determined to be necessary.

How do I Reset my Password?

  1. Make sure you are signed into your account
  2. Go toaccount.example.edu/reset.
  3. Follow all of the instructions provided
  4. Confirm the email verification link to activate your new password.
