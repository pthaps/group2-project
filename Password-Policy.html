Full Password Reset Policy

Purpose. To protect user accounts and organizational data, this policy outlines the procedures for creating, maintaining, and resetting passwords securely.

Expectations. All users must reset their passwords every 90 days. Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of upper/lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Users must initiate an immediate reset if they suspect compromise.
